Simple Process

How NearNeed Works

From signup to getting help — it takes less than 2 minutes.

1
📱

Sign Up

Register with your name, contact & address. We verify with OTP and detect your GPS location automatically.

→
2
📢

Post a Request

Describe what you need — groceries, medical help, tools, elderly care. Add urgency and optional photo.

→
3
🔔

Neighbors Are Notified

All neighbors within 1km radius receive instant push notifications and see your request on their map.

→
4
🤝

Help is Given

A neighbor clicks "I Can Help", you coordinate, the task is completed, and you both rate the experience.

Platform Features

Everything Your Community Needs

Built with real Indian neighborhoods in mind — offline-friendly, mobile-first, and completely free.

📍

1km Radar Map

See all active help requests plotted on an interactive Google Map within your 1km neighborhood radius. Color-coded by urgency.

🚨

Real-Time Notifications

Instant push notifications when a new emergency or high-priority request is posted near you. Never miss a neighbor in need.

📋

Categorized Requests

Filter by Emergency, Medical, Grocery, Household, Transport, Elderly Care, and more. Find what's relevant to you.

📣

Community Notice Board

Post Utilities alerts (water cuts, power outages), Civic notices, Social events, and Safety alerts for all nearby neighbors to see.

⭐

Ratings & Trust

Both helpers and requesters rate each other after task completion. Build trust in your community through verified reviews.

🔐

OTP Verification

Register and login with real OTP verification via Email or SMS. Your data stays secure with bcrypt password hashing.
